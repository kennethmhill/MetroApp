About The Team

We are Emily, Kenneth, and Jake, three UMD students who are passionate about public transportation! We designed MetroHelp so that DMV citizens and tourists alike have a one-stop-shop for all their Metro info needs.


With MetroHelp, You Can:

  1. Find station addresses
  2. Learn weekday and weekend opening times
  3. Catch elevator and escalator outages before you get to the station
  4. Plan your trip around system delays and station closures

Future Development

In the future, MetroHelp plans to include:

  1. Bus station information
  2. Rail station parking information
  3. Station-to-station route planning
